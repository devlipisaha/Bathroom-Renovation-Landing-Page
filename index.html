Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a bathroom renovation take?
Most standard renovations take 2–3 weeks once on-site. We provide a clear timeline upfront and keep you updated daily. We don't start until all materials are on-hand to prevent delays.
What Melbourne suburbs do you service?
We service all of Melbourne — South Yarra, Brighton, Camberwell, Richmond, Hawthorn, Kew, Toorak, St Kilda, Malvern, Prahran, and more. Contact us to confirm your suburb.
Do you provide a written fixed quote?
Always. Detailed fixed quotes in writing after our on-site visit. No vague allowances, no surprise extras. The price quoted is the price you pay — full stop.
Are you licenced and insured?
Yes. Fully licenced, insured and compliant. All trade work — plumbing, electrical, waterproofing — done by licenced professionals. Compliance certificates issued on completion.
What does the 7-year guarantee cover?
All workmanship — waterproofing, tiling, cabinetry, all fit-off work. If there's a defect within 7 years, we return and fix it at no cost to you.
How much does a Melbourne bathroom renovation cost?
Full renovations typically range from $18,000–$35,000 depending on size, scope, and finishes. We provide a detailed fixed quote after assessing your specific space — so you always know exactly what to expect.
